Visas


Every national (except Kenyans) need a visa to enter Ethiopia. Single-entry 1 -3 month tourist visas can
be issued upon arrival at the Bole International Airport in Addis Ababa.

Immunizations


A Yellow Fever vaccination certificate is no longer mandatoryin order to enter Ethiopia, but if you've
recently traveled to a country where it is present you will need proof of immunization.

Currency and Money Matters


Foreign currency is rarely used in Ethiopia, instead you'll be paying for most hotels, tours and food with
Ethiopian currency -- Birr. 1 Birr is divided into 100 cents. There are 1, 5, 10, 50 and 100 Birr notes.
The Birr is very stable and there is no significant difference between the official rate and black market rate.
Most big hotels accept debit and credit card. Make sure you have enough cash befor you leave the cities
you may not find ATM machine once you leave the cities.
