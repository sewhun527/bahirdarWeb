Welcome To Bahir Dar
Bahir Dar or Bahar Dar (Amharic: ባሕር ዳር?, Baḥər Dar, “sea shore”) is a city in north-western Ethiopia.
It is the capital of the Amhara Region (kilil). Bahir Dar is situated on the southern shore of Lake Tana,
the source of the Blue Nile (or Abay). The city is located approximately 578 km north-northwest of Addis Ababa,
with an elevation of about 1,800 meters (5,906 feet) above sea level
